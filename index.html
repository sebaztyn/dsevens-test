we are creative
onuk ink

Onuk Ink is a design firm that helps craft unique brand experiences. We push the limit of possibilities, through design. We work with amazing individuals and brands to push the limit of possibilities, engage with authenticity and refocus their brands, through design.
Onuk means "to push" and "to advance". We try to push the boundaries around design. We blend traditional art with digital skills to help you bring your Ideas to life and communicate your vision. It is our belief that your visual communication is rooted in clear thinking and rich stories.

promotional campaign

flyer design
for the roothub
uyo & portharcourt

The Roothub, a creative, business, tech hub & co-working space needed flyer designs that will get people interested in their services in their two locations. They offer trainings focused on digital skills, entrepreneurship and offer workspace for startup businesses. We decided to use an icon easily recognized by their target market- the smartphone, with bold vibrant colors to catch attention. The design was deployed in print and social media.
